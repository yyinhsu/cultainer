# Cultainer - App Store Description (English)

## App Name
Cultainer

## Subtitle
Track, Record & Discover Media

## Description
Cultainer is your personal media companion — track the books you read, movies you watch, TV shows you binge, and music you love, all in one place.

**Key Features:**

- Record and organize your media consumption (books, movies, TV, music)
- Search and auto-fill from Google Books, TMDB, and Spotify
- Save excerpts, highlights, and notes from books
- OCR text capture: photograph book pages and extract text instantly
- AI-powered analysis with Gemini: get insights, summaries, and reflections
- Explore recommendations based on creators you enjoy
- Calendar view to review your media journey over time
- Beautiful dark theme designed for comfortable reading
- Cloud sync across all your Apple devices

**Why Cultainer?**

Whether you're a voracious reader, a film enthusiast, or a music lover, Cultainer helps you remember and reflect on everything you consume. Turn passive consumption into active knowledge extraction.
